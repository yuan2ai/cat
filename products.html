Loading Guidance

Container, pallet, and mixed-SKU strategies

Container Loading Playbook

20′ loads: 13.5T net with 1,360 × 10L bags or 1,560 × 5L packs. 40′ HQ loads: 26.5T net with 2,720 × 10L or 2,960 × 5L packs.

Include slip-sheets, corner boards, and desiccant packs per lane. Palletized loads recommended for EU & UK road checks.

Mixed-Container Rules

Mix up to 6 SKUs per container with minimum 300 pack increments. Combine ready and OEM SKUs by securing shared scent loads.

Use our loading calculator to validate road-weight restrictions before booking trucks.

Wholesale & Tiered Pricing

Tier 1: 1×20′ base pricing · Tier 2: 1×40′ HQ (−3%) · Tier 3: 3×40′ HQ rolling (−6%) with 30-day replenishment SLA.
