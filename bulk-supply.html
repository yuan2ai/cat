Packaging Guidance

FIBC ton bags, valve bags, and moisture protection

FIBC Ton Bags

1,000–1,250 kg bags with UV-resistant outer, PE liner, optional foil insert, and 4-loop lifting.

Recommended for containerized export and mechanized unloading.

25/50 kg Valve Bags

Multi-wall paper or PP woven, heat-sealed inner liner, palletized with corner boards and stretch wrap.

Ideal for repackers feeding semi-automatic filling lines.
