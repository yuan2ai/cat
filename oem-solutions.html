Timeline

From brief to loading in five milestones

1. Discovery (Day 0–2)

Define target markets, SKU mix, scent direction, packaging formats, and compliance checklist.

2. Design Kit (Day 3–7)

Deliver dielines, language templates, claim library, and regulatory icons for selected regions.

3. R&D & Pilot (Day 8–20)

Run perfume/formula experiments, create printed prototypes, and share unboxing footage.

4. Production (Day 21–45)

Secure raw materials, schedule lines, monitor QA gates, and release pre-shipment reports.

5. Loading & Replenishment (Day 46+)

Container loading with live photo/video, release docs, and plan replenishment cadence.

Cost Guidance

Transparent fees and triggers

Sampling & Development

OEM-Lite: $280 deposit per SKU credited to first order. OEM-Full: $480 covers perfume R&D + printed prototype.

Expedited fragrance sourcing incurs actual courier fees.

Plate & Tooling

Charged at supplier cost (typically $600–$1,200) and refunded after three production cycles.

Bucket molds or scoop tooling quoted separately with amortization schedule.

Price Refresh

Index recalculated if bentonite cost or FX shifts ±5% or artwork change triggers re-quotation.

Quarterly price guard available with rolling 3×40′ HQ commitment.

Acceptance Standards

Quality tolerances and testing

Visual & Color

ΔE < 2 on primary colors, ≤5% variance on metallic/special finishes. Print checklists issued per batch.

Scent Consistency

Perfume load tolerance ±5%. Retention samples stored for 12 months to verify repeatability.

Granule & Performance

Particle size ±0.1 mm vs. target, absorption >300%, dust <0.25%, clump drop test >94% integrity.

Shipping Durability

Drop/compression tests to ISTA 3A standards for cartons and e-commerce ready formats.
