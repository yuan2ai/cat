Timeline

Milestones that shaped our factory

2012 — Mine Development

Secured Ningcheng sodium bentonite deposit, built ore drying and milling facilities.

2015 — First Export Containers

Launched ready-to-sell SKUs for Korea and Southeast Asia, reaching 12 countries.

2018 — OEM Expansion

Opened packaging design studio and perfume lab, enabling OEM-Lite and OEM-Full programs.

2021 — Compliance Upgrade

Implemented digital QC logging, SGS partnerships, and sustainability roadmap.

2024 — Capacity Boost

Added 3 new lines, expanded warehouse automation, and reached 62 export markets.
